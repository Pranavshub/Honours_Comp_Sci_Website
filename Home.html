In depth Curriculum

In Honours Computer Science, we provide a plethora of the very best tools and skills needed to become heavily involved in the amazing world of computer science.

Multiple Languages

In this course you will learn many different languages and skills. You will learn to code in HTML and CSS; as a result, you will know how to build a website. In addition you will learn Python one of the most used and most utilized languages in the field.

Learn to work as a team

In addition, the teacher, Dr. Lewis, is well versed in CS and you will also get to collaborate with many other students that are just excited as you are. As a result, you will gain experience with working in a team to obtain a specific goal.

State of the art computers and tools

We also have many state of the art tools used in various fields of computer science so that you can explore as many facets of this greatly interesting subject in detail.
